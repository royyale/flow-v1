# Flow — CLAUDE.md

## Project identity
Flow is an AI operating system for HR consultants and boutique people-ops teams.
It helps users manage clients, generate professional documents, surface risks, and act with clarity across a client portfolio.

## Product feeling
- Premium, calm, intelligent, and high-trust.
- Dark teal visual identity.
- Memorable, fluid, and polished.
- Feels like a control system, not a generic AI app.

## Audience
Primary audience:
- HR consultants
- People-ops advisors
- Boutique firms managing multiple clients

## Design direction
- Keep the existing brand direction and core structure mostly intact.
- Avoid generic AI startup aesthetics.
- Avoid robotic, boxy, or overly templated UI.
- Use subtle motion to create a flowing, water-like experience.

## Motion language
The motion should feel like:
- slow morphing gradients
- soft blob drift
- liquid reveal masks
- ripple-like hover states
- gentle scroll parallax
- wave-like section transitions

Motion rules:
- Elegant and restrained, not flashy.
- One signature motion idea per section/page.
- Animation should support the product story.
- Do not overwhelm the content.

## Visual rules
- Keep typography premium and readable.
- Keep spacing clean and intentional.
- Keep teal as the signature accent.
- Use distinct page moods while preserving one visual system.
- The final CTA should feel like the most refined and satisfying moment.

## What to avoid
- Generic AI landing page visuals
- Clutter
- Excessive gradients
- Overcomplicated effects
- Vibecoded or robotic feel
- Random motion that does not improve the experience

## Technical notes
- This project is Vite + React + TypeScript.
- Framer Motion is available for interactions and transitions.
- Lenis is available for smooth scrolling, if needed.
- Prefer small high-impact changes over a full redesign.
- Preserve the strongest existing sections unless there is a clear reason to revise them.

## Build priority
1. Preserve product clarity.
2. Improve memorability through motion and composition.
3. Add polish without making the site complicated.
4. Make the site feel premium, calm, and alive.
